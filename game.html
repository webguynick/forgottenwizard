How It Works
A Tesla Coil is a resonant transformer. The primary circuit (capacitor + spark gap + primary coil) stores energy, then dumps it into the secondary coil all at once.

The two coils are tuned to the same resonant frequency, causing voltage to climb to millions of volts at the toroid terminal — producing spectacular lightning arcs into the air.
